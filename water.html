DIY PROCESS

Process

  1. 1

    Prepare

    Prepare the Container – Clean and sanitize the bottle to ensure it’s safe for use.

  2. 2

    Installation

    Install the Dispensing Mechanism – Attach a tap, lever, or valve to regulate flow.

  3. 3

    Connecting

    Connect Tubing – Attach the bendy straw to direct liquid from the container to the outlet.

  4. 4

    Securing

    Secure Seals & Fasteners – Use glue to prevent leaks.

  5. 4

    Stand

    Create a Support Stand – Elevate the container for proper gravity-based dispensing.

  6. 4

    Test

    Test the System – Ensure smooth flow, adjust connections, and refine efficiency.

  7. 4

    Optimize

    Optimize for Usage – Modify the design based on convenience and accessibility.
