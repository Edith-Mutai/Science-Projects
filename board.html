Ecosystems Board Game

A fun and engaging way to learn about ecosystems and their delicate balance, while also enhancing cognitive skills and social interaction.

Know More
Let's Get Started

Game Setup

✓
Place the Game Board in the center of the table.
✓
Each player receives an Ecosystem Mat and 5 Species Cards.
✓
Shuffle the Environmental Event Deck and place it face down.
✓
Place the resource and adaptation tokens nearby.
✓
Each player starts by placing one Producer in their ecosystem.
Preview

Actions

  1. 1

    Draw Card

    Draw a Species Card: Add a new species to your hand to increase your options for building your ecosystem.

  2. 2

    Place Card

    Place a Species Card: Use resource tokens (Sunlight, Water, Nutrients) to add a new species to your ecosystem mat.

  3. 3

    Create Connection

    Create a Connection: Link two species with a food web connection (e.g., predator → prey) or a symbiotic relationship (e.g., mutualism). Strengthens the ecosystem and earns biodiversity points.

  4. 4

    Acquire Token

    Acquire an Adaptation Token: Spend resources to earn a token such as Drought Resistance, Disease Resistance, or Behavioral Adaptation. Helps species survive Environmental Events.

  5. 4

    Apply

    Apply an Adaptation to a Species: Assign an acquired adaptation token to a species in your ecosystem. Increases its survival chances and earns points.

Let's Play

Sample Solo Gameplay

1
- Started in the Grassland habitat.
- Placed Sunflower (Producer).
- Gained 1 Sunlight during the Resource Phase.
- Placed Caterpillar (Primary Consumer), forming connection: Sunflower → Caterpillar.
2
- Gain 1 Sunlight from Sunflower.
- Placed Frog (Secondary Consumer), completing food chain: Sunflower → Caterpillar → Frog.
- Drew Fern (Producer) for next round.
3
- Gain 1 Sunlight from Sunflower.
- Spent Sunlight to acquire Drought Resistance Adaptation.
- Applied Drought Resistance to Frog

Environmental Event: Drought


- The event affects Consumers in the Grassland without Drought Resistance.
- Caterpillars are removed (no adaptation).
- The frog survives due to Drought Resistance.
- The food chain is broken, and some points are lost.

Biodiversity Points Summary and How To Earn

- Before Event: 4 Points (3 species + 1 full chain)
- After Event: 2 Points (1 species removed, lost chain bonus)

+1 for each unique species in your ecosystem.
+1 per food web connection.
+2 for each complete food chain (Producer → Consumer → Decomposer).
+1 per successfully applied Adaptation Token.
Bonus points for special features like keystone species or complete nutrient cycles.

Environmental Events

Environmental Event cards are introduced every 2 rounds (or every round for more challenge).
These test the resilience of ecosystems.

Examples:
- Drought: Consumers in the Grassland/Wetland without Drought Resistance are lost.
- Disease Outbreak: Affects all zones; species without Disease Resistance may be removed.
- Floods- Species like the primary consumers drown, leading to a break in the food chain.
- Climate Change: Randomly shifts a species to an incompatible habitat.
